# Interactive Quiz Game

A modern, interactive quiz application built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Test your knowledge across various topics with a sleek, user-friendly interface.

## Features

- **Dynamic Quiz Experience**: Answer multiple-choice questions with instant feedback
- **Progress Tracking**: Visual progress bar and score display to track your performance
- **Responsive Design**: Fully responsive layout that works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- **Real-time Feedback**: Immediate visual feedback for correct and incorrect answers
- **Score Display**: Keep track of your current score throughout the quiz
- **Navigation**: Move between questions with Previous/Next buttons
- **Results Summary**: View your final score and performance message upon quiz completion
- **Review Mode**: Option to review your answers after completing the quiz
- **Restart Functionality**: Start a new quiz attempt anytime

## How to Play

1. **Start the Quiz**: Open `quiz.html` in your web browser
2. **Select Your Answer**: Click on one of the four multiple-choice options (A, B, C, or D)
3. **Submit Your Answer**: Click the "Submit Answer" button to confirm your response
4. **View Feedback**: See whether your answer was correct or incorrect
5. **Navigate Questions**: Use "Previous" and "Next" buttons to move through the quiz
6. **Complete the Quiz**: Answer all questions and view your final score and performance message
7. **Review or Restart**: Option to review answers or restart the quiz for another attempt

## Customization

To add your own questions, modify the quiz questions in `quiz.js` by updating the questions array with your custom questions and answer options.
